Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive education from early childhood to junior high

Early Childhood Education

Our nurturing program for ages 3-6 focuses on foundational skills through play-based learning, developing:

  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Social-emotional skills
  • Creativity and curiosity
  • Fine and gross motor skills
Learn More

Primary Education

Our comprehensive program for grades 1-6 builds strong academic foundations while fostering:

  • Critical thinking skills
  • STEM exploration
  • Language proficiency
  • Cultural awareness
Learn More

Junior High Academy

Our rigorous program prepares students for high school with emphasis on:

  • Advanced mathematics and sciences
  • Technology integration
  • Leadership development
  • Research and presentation skills

Curriculum Highlights

STEM Education

Our integrated STEM program combines hands-on learning with real-world applications, featuring:

  • Weekly science labs
  • Coding and robotics
  • Mathematics enrichment
  • Annual science fair

Language Arts

Our comprehensive literacy program develops strong communication skills through:

  • Phonics-based reading
  • Creative writing workshops
  • Public speaking opportunities
  • Literature appreciation

Character Education

We integrate values-based learning throughout our curriculum, focusing on:

  • Respect and responsibility
  • Community service
  • Leadership development
  • Cultural awareness

Arts & Creativity

Our arts program nurtures self-expression and creativity through:

  • Visual arts instruction
  • Music and movement
  • Drama and performance
  • Annual arts showcase

Academic Calendar

Our school year is divided into three terms with carefully planned breaks and special events:

  • Term 1: September - December
  • Term 2: January - March
  • Term 3: April - July

Key dates include parent-teacher conferences, cultural celebrations, and academic competitions.
