Join Our PTA

Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) at Honeypot Academy

At Honeypot Academy, our PTA fosters collaboration between parents and teachers to create the best learning environment for our students. Through the PTA, parents have a voice in school activities and decision-making, supporting our children's success.

What Does the PTA Do?

  • Communication: Keeps parents informed about programs, events, and updates.
  • Event Planning: Organizes sports days, cultural festivals, and fundraisers.
  • Advocacy: Provides a platform for parents to suggest ideas and improvements.
  • Support: Assists the school through volunteering and resources.

How to Be Part of the PTA

  • Attend PTA meetings to stay informed and contribute.
  • Volunteer at school events.
  • Join our PTA WhatsApp group for updates.

Your participation shapes the future of Honeypot Academy and supports your child's success!
