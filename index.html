Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) at HoneyPot Academy

At HoneyPot Academy, the Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) is a key platform that fosters collaboration between parents and teachers to create the best learning environment for our students. Through the PTA, we ensure parents have a voice in school activities and decision-making, and together, we support our children’s educational success.

What Does the PTA Do?

  • Communication: Keeps parents informed about school programs, events, and updates.
  • Event Planning: Organizes activities like sports days, cultural festivals, and fundraisers.
  • Advocacy: Provides a platform for parents to suggest ideas and improvements for the school.
  • Support: Helps the school through volunteering and offering resources.

How to Be Part of the PTA

  • Attend PTA meetings to stay informed and contribute to school decisions.
  • Volunteer to assist with school events and activities.
  • Join the PTA WhatsApp platform for easy communication and updates.

Your participation helps shape the future of HoneyPot Academy and supports your child’s success!
