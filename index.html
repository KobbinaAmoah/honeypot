Admissions Information

Welcome to Honeypot Academy

At Honeypot Academy, we believe every child deserves the best possible start in their educational journey. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing, stimulating, and safe environment where young minds can explore, learn, and grow with confidence.

Located in the heart of Adenta, Accra, our school offers a comprehensive curriculum from Kindergarten to Junior High School, designed to foster academic excellence, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning. We are more than just a school; we are a community committed to shaping well-rounded individuals prepared for a bright future.

Why Choose Honeypot Academy?

Safe & Nurturing Environment

We prioritize a secure and welcoming campus with 24 hour cctv and trained security personel where students feel respected, valued, and encouraged to express themselves freely.

STEM Focus & Holistic Growth

We emphasize Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) alongside creative arts and sports, ensuring a well-rounded education that prepares students for future challenges.

Dedicated Facilitators

Our team of experienced and passionate educators is committed to creating a supportive learning atmosphere, inspiring each child to reach their full potential with personalized attention.
