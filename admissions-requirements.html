Admission Requirements HoneyPot Academy

Prepare yourself for a successful educational journey at HoneyPot Academy.

Explore the necessary steps and documents required for admission into our academy.

Admission Requirements

We welcome students who are curious, passionate, and eager to learn. Here are the requirements for admission:

  • Completed Application Form: The first step to starting your journey at HoneyPot Academy.
  • Entrance Exam Results: The entrance assessment helps us understand your academic potential.
  • Previous Academic Records: Provide records from your previous educational institution.
  • Recommendation Letters (optional): A letter from a teacher or mentor recommending you for admission.

Once you submit the required documents and complete the necessary assessments, our team will review your application and contact you regarding the next steps.

Additional Information

In addition to the basic requirements, we have a few additional guidelines:

  • Age Requirement: Students should be within the age range for their respective grade levels.
  • Health Form: A completed health form ensuring students are fit to participate in school activities.
  • Parental/Guardian Consent: Parents or guardians must approve their child’s application and participation in the academy.

If you have any questions or need assistance with the application process, please don’t hesitate to get in touch with us. We're here to help!
