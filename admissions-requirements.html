Admission Requirements

Everything you need to know to join Honeypot Academy

General Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth application process, please review the following requirements carefully.

Application Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Medical report/immunization records
  • Transfer letter (for transferring students)

Academic Records

  • Previous school report cards (last 2 years)
  • Transcripts (for secondary school applicants)
  • Entrance examination results
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
Grade-Specific Requirements:

Nursery: Age 2-4 years
Primary: Age 5-11 years
JHS: Age 12-15 years

Additional Items

  • Completed health form
  • Parent/guardian consent form
  • Proof of residence (utility bill)
  • Application fee payment receipt
  • Special needs documentation (if applicable)

Admission Process

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form along with all required documents.

Entrance Assessment

Schedule and complete the entrance examination or interview as required for your grade level.

Application Review

Our admissions committee will review your complete application package.

Admission Decision

You'll receive notification of the admission decision within 10 business days.

Enrollment Completion

Complete registration by submitting final documents and paying tuition fees.

Important Dates

Application Deadlines
  • Early Admission: March 1 - April 30
  • Regular Admission: May 1 - July 15
  • Late Admission: July 16 - August 31 (space permitting)
Assessment Dates
  • Primary School: First Saturday of each month
  • Junior High: Second Saturday of each month
Orientation

New student orientation takes place one week before the start of each term.
