Welcome, Prospective Families!

Thank you for considering Honeypot Academy for your child's education. We understand that choosing the right school is a significant decision, and our admissions team is here to guide you every step of the way. We seek to enroll students who are curious, eager to learn, and will contribute positively to our vibrant school community.

Our admissions process is designed to be thorough yet straightforward, ensuring we get to know each applicant and family. We look for a good fit between the student, the family, and our school's values and academic environment. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive student body.

Our Admissions Process

1. Inquiry & Discovery

Explore our website, download our brochure, or contact us with any initial questions.

2. School Visit / Tour

We invite you to visit our campus, meet our team, and see our facilities. (Contact us to schedule).

3. Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with all required documents.

Go to Application

4. Assessment / Interview

Applicants may undergo a friendly assessment or interview to gauge readiness.

Joining Our Learning Community

Honeypot Academy proudly offers a comprehensive educational program for students from:

  • Kindergarten (Crèche, Nursery, KG1, KG2)
  • Lower Primary (Grade 1 - 3)
  • Upper Primary (Grade 4 - 6)
  • Junior High School (JHS 1 - 3)

We aim to make the transition into our school as smooth as possible for new students and their families. Our supportive environment helps children adapt quickly and feel at home.
