How to Apply to Honeypot Academy

  1. Step 1: Purchase and Submit the application form.
  2. Step 2: Schedule an entrance assessment.
  3. Step 3: Attend an interview (parents and student).
  4. Step 4: Receive an admissions decision.

Admission Requirements

We would love to have your curious, passionate minds eager to learn. Our admission requirements include:

  • Entrance Exam Results
